Association football, commonly known as football, or soccer, is a team sport played between
two teams of 11 players each, who primarily use their feet to propel a ball around a rectangular field
called a pitch. The objective of the game is to score more goals than the opposing team by moving
the ball beyond the goal line into a rectangular-framed goal defended by the opposing team.
Traditionally, the game has been played over two 45-minute halves, for a total match time of 90
minutes. With an estimated 250 million players active in over 200 countries and territories, it is the
world's most popular sport.
