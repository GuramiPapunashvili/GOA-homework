Re:Zero starting life in another dimension often referred to simply as Re:Zero and also known as Re: Life in a different world from zero,
is a Japanese light novel series written by Tappei Nagatsuki and illustrated by Shin'ichirō Ōtsuka.
It started serialization as a web novel on the user-generated website Shōsetsuka ni Narō in 2012.
38 light novels, as well as five side story volumes and ten short story collections have been published by Media Factory under their MF Bunko J imprint.
